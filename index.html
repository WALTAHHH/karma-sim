How to Play
☸ Karma
Karma is your spiritual currency across lifetimes. Good choices earn karma, which carries forward to improve your next life's starting conditions and unlock new experiences.
📊 Stats
Four stats shape your life:
Health - survival
Wealth - resources
Education - knowledge
Connections - relationships
🎭 Choices
Some events offer choices; others are fate. Your decisions affect your stats and can earn you character traits that shape future outcomes. Choices compound across a lifetime.
🔓 Unlocks
Spend karma between lives to unlock new countries, historical eras, and career paths. Each unlocks new events and opportunities to explore.
